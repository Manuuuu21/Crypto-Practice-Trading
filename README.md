# 🚀 Crypto Trading Simulator

A cryptocurrency trading simulator for practicing spot and futures trading with realistic candlestick charts.

**[Try it Live](https://manuuuu21.github.io/Crypto-Trading-Simulator/)**

## Features

## ✨ Features

### Trading Modes
- **Spot Trading**: Buy and sell cryptocurrencies directly with your practice balance
- **Futures Trading**: Open long or short positions with leverage (1x, 5x, 10x, 20x, 50x, 100x, 125x)

### Chart & Analysis
- **Real-time 5-Second Candlestick Charts**: Ultra-fast timeframes (5s, 15s, 30s)
- **Technical Indicators**: Moving Average (MA7) and Bollinger Bands (BB)
- **Drawing Tools**: Trendlines and rectangles for technical analysis
- **Interactive Chart**: Zoom, pan, and hover for detailed candle information
- **Order Book**: Real-time bid/ask visualization

### Order Management
- **Limit Orders**: Set custom price and amount
- **Quick Entry**: Percentage-based slider (0%, 25%, 50%, 75%, 100%)
- **Real-time P&L**: Track unrealized profit/loss on open positions
- **Liquidation Protection**: See liquidation price for futures positions
- **Trade History**: Complete log of all executed trades

### Portfolio Tracking
- **Account Balance**: Real-time USDT and BTC balance
- **Portfolio Statistics**: 
  - Total Net Value
  - Unrealized P&L
  - Total Profit/Loss Percentage
  - Liquidation Price (for futures)
  - Current Inventory

## How to Use

1. Start with 500 USDT practice balance
2. Choose **Spot** or **Futures** mode
3. Set price and amount (or use the % slider)
4. Click **Buy/Sell** to execute trades
5. For **Futures**: Select leverage and open Long/Short positions

## 💡 Trading Strategies

### Risk Management
- Start with **low leverage** (1x-5x) to understand the mechanics
- Use **stop-losses** by monitoring liquidation prices
- Don't risk more than you can afford to lose (in practice)
- Use the **percentage slider** for consistent position sizing

### Technical Analysis
- **MA(7)**: Shows 7-period moving average for trend direction
- **Bollinger Bands**: Identify overbought/oversold conditions
- **Trendlines**: Mark key support and resistance levels
- **Volume**: Analyze buying/selling pressure from volume bars

### Leverage Strategy
- **1x-5x**: Safe for beginners, lower profit potential
- **10x-20x**: Medium risk, practice position management
- **50x+**: High risk, suitable for experienced traders only
